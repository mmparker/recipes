## Traditional Cornish 

For every 2.5 cups flour:

 - 8 oz beef (sliced flap steak is a good choice, ground beef is fine)
 - 1 carrot
 - 1 parsnip
 - 1 rutabaga
 - 1 potato



## Pear Filling

For every 2.5 cups flour:

 - 2 Comice pears or 3 Bartletts
 - Nutmeg
